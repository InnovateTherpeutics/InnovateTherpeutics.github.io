MuRelief

Revolutionising Pain Management

Innovate Therapeutics is proud to introduce MuRelief, a significant advancement in the field of pain management. This novel therapeutic agent represents a paradigm shift in opioid therapy, offering effective pain relief while mitigating the risks and side effects associated with traditional opioid medications.

MuRelief is a highly selective biased agonist, meticulously designed to target the Mu-1 opioid receptor, the primary receptor responsible for mediating analgesia (pain relief). Unlike conventional opioids, which activate both Mu-1 and Mu-2 receptors indiscriminately, MuRelief's unique mechanism of action preferentially activates the pain-relieving signalling pathways associated with Mu-1, while simultaneously avoiding the activation of Mu-2 receptors.

The activation of Mu-2 receptors is known to induce a range of adverse effects, including respiratory depression, addiction, dependence, and severe gastrointestinal disturbances. By selectively targeting Mu-1, MuRelief offers patients:

  • Superior Pain Relief: MuRelief provides robust and reliable pain management, comparable to traditional opioids, enabling patients to experience significant relief from acute and chronic pain conditions
  • Reduced Risk of Addiction and Dependence:The selective activation of Mu-1 receptors minimises the risk of developing addiction and dependence, enhancing patient safety and promoting responsible pain management.
  • Minimised Respiratory DepressionBy avoiding Mu-2 receptor activation, MuRelief significantly reduces the risk of respiratory depression, a potentially life-threatening side effect associated with traditional opioids.
  • Improved Gastrointestinal TolerabilityMuRelief exhibits a lower incidence of constipation, nausea, and other gastrointestinal side effects compared to traditional opioids, improving patient comfort and adherence to therapy
  • Enhanced Quality of Life and Functional AbilityBy effectively managing pain without the debilitating side effects of traditional opioids, MuRelief enables patients to maintain their quality of life, engage in daily activities, and return to normal function more quickly, promoting overall well-being.
